Creative writing

Since 2021, I have been part of Atrapavientos, an association working towards the promotion of reading and creative writing focused on young literature both in Spain and internationally. Here you can find come nice projects we have done!
Creative Writing with AI

For the `Tour del Talento´ we proposed a session where we used ChatGPT and other tools to solve the writers block, while also discussing the advantages, challenges and risks of using AI in creative processes Read more..

LuchaLibro

5 minutes, 3 words. A creative writing competition where your words are stronger than your fists. Funded by the Spanish Ministry of Education and in collaboration with Fundación Ibercaja Read more..

Plantadores de historias

Under the lemma: 'stories need to be taken care of to grow', we work with memories and wishes, share them, write them down in seeded paper and bury them in the ground to let a plant flourish from them. Read more..

Libros que Importan
