About
Predoctoral researcher at University of Zaragoza

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. about metaphor identification and interpretation using Language Models and Linked Data advised by Dr. Jorge Gracia and Dr. Jorge Bernad and as part of SID research group at the university of Zaragoza. Before that I did my Masters on Theoretical and Applied Linguistic at Pompeu Fabra University and my BA in Spanish Language and Literature at the University of Zaragoza. In the past couple of years I was lucky enough to do an internship at Brainy, where I could apply my theoretical knowledge to the company, developing NLP microservices. And I also collaborate with Atrapavientos, giving creative writing workshops.
